once youre done downloading audiostreamer from:
https://piotrekunitydeveloper.github.io/AudioStreamer/
you can create a content folder which can hold a music, all of its instrumental tracks
and its lyrics.

Lyrics Template

in order:

16 - seconds at which the lyric part should be triggered
+pause+ (interval in seconds) - pause in lyrics (can be used for long pauses thoughout the song)
She say im a tandrum - the actual part of lyrics

Example:

16 she say im a tandrum
19 eighteenth's year behind me
22 sup yall
+pause+ 6
29 thats some lyrics here

Please check out the examples provided in Documents/AudioStreamer/songs
